About the Conference

The International Conference on Computer Science, Engineering, and Technology is designed to serve individuals engaged in the fields of Computer Science, Engineering, and Technology. It offers a chance for personal growth and community involvement. This event will take place in a hybrid format, enabling participation either in person in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, or virtually from your home or workplace.
